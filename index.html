About the club

Tennis for the
medical community

Max's Tennis is an informal club for medics, doctors, students, and healthcare workers who share a love of the game. Whether you're a seasoned club player or haven't picked up a racket since uni, all levels are welcome.

We meet regularly across London, keep things relaxed and social, and believe the best post-call recovery is a solid set on a sunny afternoon.

Open to all levels & specialties
